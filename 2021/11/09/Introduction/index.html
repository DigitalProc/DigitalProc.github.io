Introduction

欢迎大家选修高老师的《工程信号处理》。

Welcome to the course website of “Digital Signal Processing” by Professor Gao.

搭建这个网站的初衷是为同学们开辟一个课堂之外的知识交流平台，为大家提供知识、代码的交流渠道。

The original intention of building this website is to establish a knowledge exchanging platform outside the classroom for students. Sharing scope includes but not limited to knowledge and code.

希望能够在学习的过程中给同学们一些帮助，在课程结束之后让同学们有所收获。

Hope you enjoy it! Have fun!
